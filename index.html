Common Questions, Straight Answers

Everything you need to know about getting covered.

How much does life insurance cost?
Costs vary based on your age, health, coverage amount, and the type of policy you choose. However, term life insurance is often much more affordable than people think—many healthy adults in their 30s can secure substantial coverage for less than a dollar a day.
Do I need a medical exam?
Not necessarily. We offer many "no-exam" options where approval is based on a quick health questionnaire and electronic record checks. If an exam is required to get you the best rate, we arrange it at your convenience, usually in your own home.
What's the difference between term and whole life?
Term life covers you for a specific period (like 20 years) and pays out if you pass away during that time; it's the most affordable option. Whole life covers you for your entire life, never expires as long as premiums are paid, and includes a cash value component that grows over time.
How long does it take to get coverage?
With modern, simplified underwriting, many of our clients are approved and covered within 48 hours of their application. Fully underwritten policies (requiring an exam) typically take 3 to 6 weeks.
Can I change my policy later?
Yes. Life changes, and your coverage should too. Many term policies have options to convert to permanent coverage later without another health exam. You can also typically decrease your coverage amount or apply for additional coverage as needed.
Is my consultation really free?
Absolutely. Our consultations are 100% free and carry zero obligation. Our goal is to provide you with the information you need to make the best decision for your family, whether you choose to purchase through us or not.
